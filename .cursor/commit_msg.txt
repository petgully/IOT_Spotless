Phase 2: kiosk refactor to integration contract v1.1

Implements docs/INTEGRATION_CONTRACT.md v1.1 end-to-end on the kiosk side.
The kiosk now resolves bookings to (size, package, addons), tracks every
second of relay-on time per stage, persists progress locally + to cloud,
and resumes interrupted sessions after power loss without giving away
free service.

Modules changed:
- session_stages.py:  rewritten around build_session(size, package, addons).
  SET A / SET B size profiles, disinfectant always included, med_bath
  swaps shampoo pump p1->p3, extra_dry adds +5min or runs standalone.
  Adds 'accounting' field per stage (relays vs wallclock).
- config_manager.py:  drops session_types block; introduces size_profiles
  (A,B) + cloud_sync + resume sub-configs. Migrates old configs forward.
- spotless_controller.py:  StageExecutor rewritten with per-second
  accounting, soft (2s) + hard (10s) relay-confirmation timeouts, resume
  support that skips completed stages and rewinds the current stage to
  its already-delivered count, and per-stage budget cap (anti-fraud).
- session_runner.py:  drives fresh + resume flows; wires SessionProgressStore
  (local SQLite) and CloudSyncQueue (RDS retries) into the executor's
  callbacks. Cloud writes restricted to the 9 major stages from §8.5.
- db_bookings.py:  Query A + Query B + booking_sessions writers (§8.1-§8.6)
  plus apply_cloud_op() dispatcher used by cloud_sync.
- qr_validator.py:  rewritten as a 7-gate flow (§7.3) returning
  start | resume | refuse. Booking codes routed through validate_booking_qr;
  service-mode prefixes (TEST/DEMO/DRY/...) routed through validate_test_prefix.
- kiosk/web_server.py:  routes use the new validator + machine_id; surfaces
  recovery_pending banner (§9.1) and cloud-sync degraded flag; builds
  ResumeState from local SQLite (preferred) or cloud completed_stages CSV.
- main.py:  initialises SessionProgressStore + CloudSyncQueue, runs the
  boot-recovery check at startup, propagates recovered_session to the
  kiosk UI.

New modules:
- session_progress.py:  SQLite store for the data/session_state.db ledger;
  per-second / per-5s flush APIs, dedup CSV completion, boot recovery.
- cloud_sync.py:  background async queue with exponential backoff and
  on-disk persistence at data/cloud_write_queue.json.

Tooling:
- .gitignore: excludes raspberry_pi/data/ (SQLite + queue JSON) and the
  scratch .cursor/commit_msg.txt + tmp_*.py files used during dev.
